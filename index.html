About the Author

♦

Rubina Ramesh is a romance author who writes heartfelt Indian love stories across contemporary, romantic comedy, royal, and historical settings.

Her stories explore love in all its forms — light and humorous, emotional and reflective, and sometimes shaped by destiny and time.

From feel-good romantic comedies to second-chance romances and sweeping royal and historical love stories, her books are rooted in strong emotions, relatable characters, and cultural depth. She is especially drawn to themes of reunion, healing, resilience, and the quiet strength of love that finds its way back.

Rubina's novels are written for readers who enjoy immersive storytelling, emotionally satisfying journeys, and characters who linger long after the last page. When she's not writing, she enjoys reading, observing human relationships, and finding inspiration in everyday moments.

~ Rubina
